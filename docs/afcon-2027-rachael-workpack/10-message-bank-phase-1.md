These messages should be personalised before sending. They should not suggest that Rachael has secured any AFCON role. They should position her as a serious creative professional available for discussion, collaboration, advisory input, learning, attachment, shadowing or pilot development.

## 10. Important Sending Rule

Every message should include one clear ask only. The first-stage ask should usually be:

- Who is the right person to speak with?
- Can we schedule a short meeting?
- Can Rachael share her profile?
- Is there room for attachment, shadowing, advisory discussion or pilot concept development?
