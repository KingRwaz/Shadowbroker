# First-Stage Data Index

Purpose: This file defines the first-stage data needed to move the AFCON 2027 Rachael Nanyonjo workpack from strategy into execution.

The first stage is not about applying blindly for jobs. It is about building evidence, mapping stakeholders, opening conversations, tracking responses and identifying where Rachael can add value.

---

## Stage 1 Objective

Build a verified, usable data foundation for the first 30 to 90 days of outreach.

The immediate goal is to create enough structure to support:

1. Warm introductions.
2. Formal outreach.
3. Meeting preparation.
4. Stakeholder tracking.
5. Portfolio strengthening.
6. Concept note refinement.
7. Cafe-arts learning.
8. Pilot activation design.

---

1. `data/stakeholder-map-phase-1.csv`

4. `data/pilot-activation-options.csv`
   - Early pilot concepts that can be developed into small event proposals or cafe-arts/tourism activations.

5. `data/portfolio-asset-inventory.csv`
   - Checklist of evidence Rachael should gather before and during outreach.

6. `data/cafe-arts-research-log.csv`
   - Field research tracker for studying venues, visitor experience, pricing, events and hospitality lessons for the future cafe arts space.

7. `data/opportunity-risk-register.csv`
   - Risk register to protect the project from vague promises, misinformation, unpaid exploitation and weak follow-through.

## First-Stage Data Principles

1. Do not invent contacts.
   - Names, phone numbers and emails must only be added after verification.

2. Separate confirmed information from recommendations.
   - Institution names and public roles can be listed as targets, but contact persons should remain blank until verified.

3. Track every conversation.
   - Every WhatsApp, email, call, meeting and referral should be recorded.

4. Ask for access first.
   - The first-stage ask should be meeting, guidance, attachment, advisory discussion, volunteering, shadowing, pilot partnership or referral.

5. Build proof weekly.
   - Each week should produce a better profile, better concept note, new contact, new learning note, new portfolio item or new referral.

---

## Phase 1 Success Indicators

By the end of the first 30 days:

- One-page profile completed.
- Formal letter completed.
- Concept note completed.
- Stakeholder map expanded to at least 50 targets.
- At least 20 outreach attempts recorded.
- At least 5 follow-up conversations started.
- At least 2 meetings requested or scheduled.
- At least 1 small event, cultural venue or tourism experience observed and logged.

By the end of 90 days:

- At least 50 stakeholder records completed.
- At least 5 serious meetings held.
- At least 2 practical events or cultural/tourism experiences documented.
- At least 1 pilot partner identified.
- At least 1 refined concept note prepared for a specific institution or sponsor.
- Portfolio strengthened with evidence, testimonials or documented work.

---

## Immediate Operating Rule

Rachael should be introduced as a professional creative contributor, not as a beginner looking for any opportunity. The data layer should therefore support a credible senior-level conversation while still leaving room for learning, attachment and sector observation where useful.
