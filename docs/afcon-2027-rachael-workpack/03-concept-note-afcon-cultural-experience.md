# Concept Note: AFCON 2027 Uganda Cultural Experience Programme

## 1. Title

**AFCON 2027 Uganda Cultural Experience Programme**

---

## 2. Concept Summary

The AFCON 2027 Uganda Cultural Experience Programme is a proposed creative and tourism activation designed to showcase Uganda's culture to visiting football fans, teams, officials, sponsors, media, diaspora guests and tourists.

The programme connects football with music, dance, theatre, fashion, food, crafts, storytelling, youth performance, hospitality and cafe-style cultural spaces. It would help Uganda present itself not only as a football host, but as a culturally rich, welcoming and creative destination.

---

## 3. Strategic Rationale

AFCON 2027 will not only be a football tournament. It will also be a regional visibility moment for Uganda, Kenya and Tanzania. Visitors will need more than stadium access. They will need memorable experiences, safe venues, cultural interpretation, hospitality, food, music, art, guided movement and a clear sense of place.

Uganda can use AFCON 2027 to strengthen its tourism brand, create income opportunities for artists and cultural businesses, support youth performers, deepen diaspora engagement and build long-term cultural economy value.

---

## 4. Programme Objectives

1. Showcase Ugandan culture to visiting fans, tourists, media, sponsors and diaspora guests.
2. Improve visitor experience through curated cultural and hospitality activations.
3. Create opportunities for artists, dancers, performers, designers, food entrepreneurs, guides and event professionals.
4. Connect football, tourism, culture, hospitality and the creative economy.
5. Build a legacy model for future cafe-arts, performance and cultural tourism spaces.

---

## 5. Proposed Components

### 5.1 Uganda Cultural Welcome Night

A curated evening featuring dance, music, fashion, food, storytelling and visual art for visitors, sponsors, diaspora guests and local partners.

### 5.2 Fan-Zone Cultural Programming

Short, energetic performances and cultural moments integrated into fan zones, viewing areas and public gathering spaces.

### 5.3 Hotel and VIP Hospitality Activations

Premium cultural showcases for hotels, sponsors, visiting delegations, media and corporate guests.

### 5.4 Diaspora Creative Reception

A networking and cultural evening for Ugandans abroad, East African diaspora visitors, creative entrepreneurs and tourism partners.

### 5.5 Youth Performance Mentorship

Training and rehearsal support for selected youth dancers, performers and cultural groups to improve stage discipline and professional readiness.

### 5.6 Cafe Arts Pop-Up

A small temporary cultural hospitality space combining coffee, Ugandan food bites, acoustic music, visual art, storytelling, crafts and visitor orientation.

### 5.7 Football Meets Culture Showcase

A curated showcase blending football identity with cultural performance, fashion, music and storytelling.

## 11. Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: Relationship Building and Scoping

Meet tourism, culture, sports, hotel, event and sponsor contacts. Understand official AFCON channels and identify where cultural programming may be needed.

### Phase 2: Concept Refinement

Adapt the concept to match real institutional needs, budgets, venues and official guidelines.

### Phase 3: Pilot Activation

Deliver one small cultural evening, cafe arts pop-up or youth showcase before AFCON as a proof of concept.

### Phase 4: AFCON Activation

Support selected official or partner-led events during the tournament period.

### Phase 5: Legacy Report

Document lessons for Uganda's tourism, creative economy and future cafe-arts/hospitality spaces.

---

## 12. Reality Check

This concept is a proposal and should not be presented as an approved AFCON programme unless formally adopted by the relevant authorities or partners.

All branding, access to official AFCON assets, stadium spaces, fan zones and public activations must be approved through official channels.
